Precautions: A Guide to Staying Safe During the Pandemic

2020 is the year that no one will forget. However, to keep ourselves safe it's extrememly important to follow rules recommended by your county! These are some of the most basic examples you must keep in mind, so please share to everyone.

1. Wear A Mask!

It's super important that you make sure to wear a mask everytime you go out! It is also important to wear it properly! Here are some of the important takeaways:

  • Do not touch the inside of a mask
  • Make sure that the mask covers the brim of the nose and to under your chin
  • Touch your mask and face as little as possible! Only touch the ties to adjust the mask.
  • Always wash your hands before and after touching the mask.

2. Wash your hands for 20 seconds!


Make sure to wash your hands with soap and water often. Don't forget the 20 second rule! Why 20 seconds? We need this time to thoroughly remove the dirt from our hands.

3. Practice Social Distancing!

Stay at least 6 feet apart from anyone outside. This also mean, do not shake hands! Even if its just a friend, keep these rules in mind. Once again, we cannot emphasize how essential it is to follow these rules! It's for the better of your health, along with your loved ones'.

These are the most basic, essential tips to follow during this time. For further information, check out your local CDC's recommendations!
